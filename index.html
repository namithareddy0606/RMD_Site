About The Event

The objective of the symposium is to bring the young budding engineering graduates together to present their findings and discuss future trends, directions and new challenges in the Electronics & Communication field. This symposium aims to intensify the information exchange of their ideas in theoretical research and practical developments.

Technical Event & Rules

Here is our event details

DE PAPER FIESTA

A maximum of 2 members and a minimum of 1 participant are allowed
It should be in IEEE format with a maximum of two pages
Hard copy is mandatory
Soft copy should be mailed to mailto:saankethika2k22@gmail.com
Paper must be presented for 8 minutes and 2 minutes are allocated for Q&A session
TOPICS are: IOT, Robotics, AR-VR, 6G Technologies, Sustainability, Biochio, Nano Electronics

THE PROJECT PARAOHS

A maximum of 3 members
Ideas in any domain are welcome
A PPT along with a working model should be mailed to saankethika2k22@gmail.com
Project details should be explained before the juries

CIRCUTRIX

A maximum of 2 members and a minimum of 1 participant are allowed
Prelims will be conducted( Questions will be given based on circuits)
Top 5 teams will be moved to FINALS
Based on time taken by the team to complete the circuits will be awarded accordingly

BRAIN TEASER

Team should contain a maximum of 2 members
Rapid quiz rounds are conducted

Non-Technical Event & Rules

Here is our event details

MEME DESIGN

This is an individual event
Time duration for the event is 30minutes
Participants can create any number of memes during the given period
They can also have the templates ready, but the content must be created on the spot
NOTE: Participants need to bring their own laptop to participate in the contest

HUNT THE HINT

This is a team event with maximum of 2 participants
Prelims will be conducted
Top 5 teams will be moved to FINALS
In finals hints will be given about the personalities and they have to find the personality
The group which guesses the correct answer in minimum number of hints gets the maximum points
